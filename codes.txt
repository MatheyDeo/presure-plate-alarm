data codes (for debuging)
these codes are messages sent betweeb the devices 

=================== codes from the scale
S10 - OK unlocked mode
S11 - OK locked mode (+offset)
S12 - OK Intruder detected (+offset)
S13 - OK Tared (+caldata)

=================== codes from the alarm
A10 - OK Alarm Online

=================== codes from the controller
G10 - OK controller online

G30 - Scale Tare & lock
G31 - Scale Unlock
G33 - Lock & Stop the alarm
G34 - Trigger the alarm
